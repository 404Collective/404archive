About the Initiative

Project Paradise is a collaborative program between the ATLYS Corporation and municipal partners to repurpose non-navigable spatial regions into livable districts. Initiated as a pilot program in 2041, Initiative 04-B focuses on ethically-guided habitation, rehabilitation services, and long-term stabilization.

Containment & Safety

All sectors are monitored by ATLYS Control teams via advanced monitoring stations. Access is restricted to authorized residents and support personnel.

Community Integration

Residents are enrolled into integration programs and have scheduled access to municipal services and vocational training.

Transparency

Detailed environmental data and program reports are available to enrolled citizens upon request and review.
